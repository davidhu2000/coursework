Ingredients

  • frozen strawberries
  • frozen blueberries
  • frozen pinapple
  • one cup of milk
  • one banana
  • plain yogurt
  • half cup of water
  • optional: kiwi

Fruit Smooth

Description

The fruit smoothie is a perfect blend of fruits and milk for a delicious cold treat.

Direction

  1. Defrost the frozen strawberries, blueberries, and pinapple for two minutes
  2. Add banana, milk, and yogurt
  3. Add water.
  4. Use blender to mix the ingredients.
  5. Enjoy the treat.
